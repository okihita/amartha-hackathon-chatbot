How It Works: The Competition Milestones
This hackathon is a progressive challenge. Only the top teams will advance to the final in-person event.

Phase 1: Registration & Ideation (Nov 5 - Nov 14) Register your team and submit your initial project concept. This is your chance to impress the judges with your idea's novelty, feasibility, and clarity.

Milestone 1: Top 30 Announced (Nov 19) The Top 30 teams will be selected from the initial concepts to proceed to the next round.

Milestone 2: Top 15 Announced (Nov 27) The Top 30 teams will submit their detailed technical architecture and business proposals. From these, the Top 15 Finalists will be chosen.

Day 2 (Nov 29): Final Demos & Judging All 15 teams will present their final, working solutions to a distinguished panel of judges. Following the demos, the judges will select the Top 3 Finalists.
