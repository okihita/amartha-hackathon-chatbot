## Executive Summary

UMKM Assistant is a **WhatsApp-based AI chatbot** that helps microfinance institutions:
- **Reduce loan defaults** through proactive financial education
- **Lower operational costs** by automating customer engagement
- **Increase loan portfolio** with better credit scoring

**Investment**: Rp 500M - 1.5B (depending on scope)  
**Expected ROI**: 300-500% within 12 months

---

## The Problem

| Challenge | Impact on OCBC |
|-----------|----------------|
| High NPL rates in UMKM segment | Rp billions in write-offs annually |
| Expensive field agent operations | Rp 50-100M/month per branch |
| Low financial literacy among borrowers | Higher default probability |
| Limited customer touchpoints | Missed upsell opportunities |

---

## The Solution

### UMKM Assistant Features

| Feature | Business Value |
|---------|----------------|
| **WhatsApp AI Chatbot** | 24/7 customer support at 1/100th the cost |
| **15-Week Financial Literacy Program** | Educated borrowers = Lower default rates |
| **AI Credit Scoring (A-Score)** | Real-time risk assessment from engagement data |
| **Business Photo Analysis** | Verify business health via image AI |
| **Real-time Admin Dashboard** | Instant visibility for loan officers |

### Why WhatsApp?
- **95% penetration** in Indonesia
- **Zero friction** - no app download needed
- **Works on basic phones** - reaches rural UMKM
