# Claude Refinement Prompt for Business Model Canvas

Copy and paste this entire prompt to Claude Sonnet 4.5:

---

I need you to transform this Business Model Canvas into a **persuasive, winning, exciting, and entertaining** 2-page document for a hackathon submission. The document should captivate judges, demonstrate commercial viability, and showcase massive social impact.

## Your Task:

Transform the attached Business Model Canvas into a compelling narrative that:

1. **Hooks immediately** - Start with a powerful statistic or story that makes judges lean in
2. **Tells a story** - Weave data and facts into a compelling narrative about transformation
3. **Shows inevitability** - Make it clear this solution WILL succeed and scale
4. **Demonstrates urgency** - Why this needs to happen NOW
5. **Balances emotion and logic** - Touch hearts while convincing minds
6. **Uses powerful language** - Active voice, strong verbs, vivid imagery
7. **Stays concise** - Maximum 2 pages, every word counts

## Specific Requirements:

### Page 1: The Opportunity & Solution
- **Opening Hook** (1 paragraph) - Grab attention with the problem's magnitude
- **Value Proposition** (2-3 paragraphs) - What we do and why it's revolutionary
- **Revenue Model** (1 paragraph + visual table) - How we make money, clearly and confidently
- **Key Resources** (1 paragraph) - What makes us uniquely positioned to win

### Page 2: Impact & Scale
- **Market Impact** (2-3 paragraphs) - The transformation we'll create
- **Scale Potential** (1 paragraph + visual) - From 10K to 1M users
- **Competitive Advantages** (bullet points) - Why we'll dominate
- **The Ask & Vision** (closing paragraph) - Inspiring call to action

## Style Guidelines:

**DO:**
- Use powerful statistics: "64 million entrepreneurs", "$165 billion credit gap"
- Paint vivid pictures: "A mother in rural Java checking her phone at 10 PM..."
- Show confidence: "We WILL reach 1 million users" not "We hope to..."
- Use active voice: "We transform" not "Transformation is enabled"
- Create urgency: "The window is NOW" "First-mover advantage"
- Balance data with humanity: Mix numbers with real impact stories
- Use formatting: **Bold** for emphasis, tables for clarity, bullets for impact

**DON'T:**
- Use jargon or buzzwords without explanation
- Make it sound like every other pitch
- Be overly technical (save that for the tech spec)
- Undersell the opportunity or impact
- Use passive voice or weak language
- Make it boring or predictable

## Tone:

- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Ambitious** but grounded in reality
- **Passionate** but professional
- **Data-driven** but human-centered
- **Visionary** but practical

## Key Messages to Emphasize:

1. **Massive Untapped Market** - 64M UMKMs, $165B credit gap, 70% women
2. **Technology Advantage** - Google Gemini AI + WhatsApp = unbeatable combination
3. **Proven Partner** - Amartha's trust and existing network
4. **Real Impact** - Not just profit, but poverty reduction and empowerment
5. **Scalability** - 10x growth with minimal cost increase
6. **First-Mover** - No competition, clear path to market leadership

## Visual Elements to Include:

1. **Revenue Progression Table** - Show Year 1-3 growth clearly
2. **Impact Metrics Box** - Highlight key numbers (users, loans, jobs created)
3. **Scale Timeline** - Visual showing 10K → 50K → 200K → 1M users

## Example Opening (for inspiration):

She's one of 64 million Indonesian entrepreneurs who contribute 61% of GDP but can't access the financial education or credit they desperately need. Until now."

## Example Closing (for inspiration):

"We're not building a chatbot. We're building the financial operating system for Indonesia's informal economy. With Google's AI, Amartha's trust, and WhatsApp's reach, we will empower 1 million entrepreneurs, create 2 million jobs, and unlock $50 billion in economic activity. The technology is ready. The market is waiting. The time is now. Let's transform microfinance together."
