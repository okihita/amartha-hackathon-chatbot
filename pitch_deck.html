Root Causes of High Default Rates

  • Information Asymmetry - Borrowers don't understand loan terms, interest calculation, or consequences of late payment
  • Poor Financial Planning - No distinction between personal and business cash flow
  • Limited Touchpoints - Only interact during collection, not proactive engagement
  • Reactive Collections - Issues discovered only after payment is missed
  • High Cost to Serve - Field agents expensive but necessary for relationship
  • No Early Warning - Can't predict which borrowers are at risk

"Financial institutions lose an average of Rp 15-20 million per defaulted UMKM loan. Preventing just one default per month per branch could save Rp 180-240 million annually."

How It Works

1

User Onboarding via WhatsApp

Borrower texts the bot, registers with name and business details. No app download required. Works on any phone.

2

Gamified Financial Education

15-week curriculum with weekly quizzes. Topics: budgeting, savings, loan management, business planning. 100% pass rate required.

3

A-Score Credit Assessment

AI calculates creditworthiness based on: engagement frequency, quiz performance, business photo analysis, payment behavior simulation.

Admin Dashboard & Alerts

Field agents and loan officers see real-time user engagement, risk scores, and early warning indicators. Prioritize outreach effectively.
